Who we are

Welcome to Coffict - the place, where you can find the best coffee in the world. Our staff will welcome you with a huge smile and will bring you the best experience possible. Coffict is a favourite place inbetween work and home, for any kind of meetings or business arrangements. Coffict is an American company for coffee and a chain of coffee shops, fond in 1971 in Seatle, USA. Currently we have more than 27 thousand coffee shops in more than 70 countries.

What we offer

In coffict you can enjoy a freshly-made aromatic cup of coffee - 100% Arabica, or a cup of tea in a cozy atmosphere, with nice music and Internet access. We also offer unique types of coffee beans, which you can use to make your own coffee at home, as well as original cups and tumblers. The lovers of healthy lifestyle can find delicious breakfasts, sandwiches, smoothies and many more great things in our stores.

Our famous Flat White turns ten

10 years ago, our Flat White took this coffee-loving nation by storm. A velvety smooth storm that’s still going strong today. Just one of the many reasons why you've kept us as your favourite coffee shop for the 10th year running.
